Metallica Fan Webpage

Metallica is an American heavy metal band that formed in 1981. The band's original members include James Hetfield, Lars Ulrich, and soon after they were joined by Kirk Hammett and Cliff Burton. Over the years, while the lineup changed slightly, their music continued to resonate with fans worldwide, giving them the title of one of the greatest metal bands of all time.
